About


Yuho Kim (1992.y) Studies and lives in Gyeonggi-do, S.Korea.


Yuho Kim explores a diversity of moments from old-fashioned moment to progressiveness.
He often views mediums and materials from the analogue way and seeks the different perspective of typography, still image and digital moving image. His works are both personal and impersonal features to carry out visual composition universally.


Yuho Kim is born in South Korea and studied in Arnhem, the Netherlands previously. He is currently an ongoing studies at Kangnam University of Universal Visual Design B.A course in S.Korea.
