Big Gesture

Big Gesture


The movie poster is everywhere. But no one do not closely see the posters. Movie poster represents what does movie like. It is important to deliver message by one poster. I thought movie poster is limited to show all of contents itself. Thus,movie poster need to change through the moving image not to use still image.

I selected the India independent movie called “Bajrangi Bhaijaan” 2015. The storyline was that main actor who has a deaf look for her mother. But, she never speak until end movie and only use the body gesture to tell what she wants to do.

Overall the movie, I thought her body gesture should stand out from the main feature. Therefore I designed the moving image of movie poster that is implemented with afterimage and glich effect. The moving image has contented a tons of information. So that I want to deliver the full message to viewer by the digital movie poster.
