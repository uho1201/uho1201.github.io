Emotional Numbers

Emotional Numbers


As water boils at 100 degree, emotions burst out at some point. I thought that this moment is similar with that of water boiling point, I started to do this.

I drew various patterns of water. After drawing or printing flow of water on each different transparent paper, I put them together.

Observation of bubble shapes upon water temperature.

I could find one size from overlapped part of oval shape in bubble, and aligned them in consecutive to make numbers.

I composed various screens using designs of “bubble, wave, and typo numbers’.

Designing water typography is designed by Flash. I chose the old method making flim each frame. Because it is fundamental emotional typeface that is abstract. Thus, It represents a "Each frame of my emotional levels".
