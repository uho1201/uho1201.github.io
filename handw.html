Good Handwriting

Good Handwriting


I used to have a bad hand-writing. When I as little, I had learned a writing language at first. The way of writing , I followed to copy each single letter for multiple times until getting better as good handwriting format. As my weak point, I want to change the improvement my hand-writing.

As far as I remember I tried to use different pens in order to have a good hand-writing. Among the different type of pen, I loved to use a thin brush pen(Chinese ink).

I practiced to write letters on the paper which is printed “Palatino type-face” with grey colour.

As you can see A to Z letter, I wrote them as 10 steps each letter and the single alphabet is going to better than before(1 step letter shape). This is the way of hand-writing how to learn I am.

After all letters, I transformed the “Digital Type-face” and I named it “Yuho hand writing” font-family. This font is placed on the digital surface as my hand-writing.
