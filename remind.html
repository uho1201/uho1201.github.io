Reminding Work Out

Reminding Work Out


when I was middle and high school student, I was interested with learning English. During school year , I found the indie band group “ELLEGARDEN” Japanese singer who speak English fluently made a lot of songs.

I was obsessed with this group and I tried to remember his lyrics. What I did is that I used lyrics as Konglish(an English word coined in Korea) then memorizing all songs.

At this time, I found the English words book which is printed English words next to Korea translated. This book is something special because it has a color transparent sheet (made of regenerated cellulose) and it can hide Korea translated words that is printed same color as transparent sheet.

For this concept, I decided to make a handy English book about remembering ELLEGARDEN lyrics with multiple ways such as hiding lyrics and read Konglish get familiar English.

This book contents ELLEGARDEN 10 different albums and each song with English and Konglish version. I designed this book by Japanese bookbinding method.
